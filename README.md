### Hey, I’m Mike.

I build things for people who need them and sometimes for the ones who don’t know they need them yet.

I spent over a decade working with veterans in jails, transitional housing, and permanent supportive housing — everything from helping folks find a place to live to acting as an SSA Rep Payee. Trauma-informed care, PTSD, addictions — that work shaped how I see systems and the people stuck inside them.

These days I’m a snowboard instructor and full-stack web developer living in upstate New York. I build tools that solve real problems I’ve seen firsthand, and I’m not great at leaving things broken when I think I can help.

---

#### What I’m Working On

- **[Shred Gremlins](https://shredgremlins.com)** — A platform connecting snowsports students, instructors, and mountains so lessons don’t start from scratch every time. The biggest problem in snow sports education is continuity, and this is my answer to it.

- **[Veteran Connector](https://veteran-connector.org)** — A veteran support platform. Took me six years to finish the submissions portal. It’s done now.

- **[All Faiths on Fire](https://allfaithsonfire.org)** — An interfaith community space. Functional, but still needs a lot of writing.

- **[EF-Vita](https://ef-vita.net)** — Multi-tenant managed hosting for Eve Frontier players who want to run a tribe without dealing with code, databases, or infrastructure. I make it easy and cheap.
